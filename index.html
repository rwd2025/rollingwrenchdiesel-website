FAQ

Common questions

Do you offer mobile service?

Yes. We offer mobile repair and roadside service calls, including weekend 24-hour service calls when available.

What should I send before a service call?

Send location, phone number, unit number, VIN if available, photos, fault codes, engine make, truck model, and a short description of the problem.

Do you work on aftertreatment systems?

Yes. We handle regen issues, DEF/SCR/DPF concerns, derates, sensor diagnostics, wiring checks, and related repairs.

Do you work on trailers?

Yes. Trailer brakes, lights, wiring, ABS, air lines, suspension, wheel seals, and general repairs.

Yes. WESS detailing and window tinting are available by appointment.
